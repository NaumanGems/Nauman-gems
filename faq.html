Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our jewelry and services

Product Information

What materials do you use in your jewelry?

We use only the finest materials including 14k and 18k gold, sterling silver, platinum, and certified diamonds and gemstones. All our metals are hypoallergenic and suitable for sensitive skin.

Are your diamonds certified?

Yes, all our diamonds come with GIA (Gemological Institute of America) certification, ensuring their quality, cut, color, clarity, and carat weight are accurately documented.

Do you offer custom jewelry design?

Yes, we offer custom design services. Our master jewelers can work with you to create a unique piece that perfectly matches your vision. Contact us for a consultation.

How do I determine my ring size?

You can visit any local jeweler for a professional sizing, or use our online ring size guide. We also offer free ring sizers that we can mail to you upon request.

Ordering & Payment

What payment methods do you accept?

We accept all major credit cards (Visa, MasterCard, American Express), PayPal, Apple Pay, Google Pay, and bank transfers for orders over $1,000.

Is my payment information secure?

Absolutely. We use industry-standard SSL encryption and PCI DSS compliance to ensure your payment information is completely secure.

Do you offer financing options?

Yes, we offer 0% financing for 12 months on purchases over $500 through our partner financing company. Apply at checkout.

Can I modify or cancel my order?

Orders can be modified or cancelled within 2 hours of placement. After that, please contact our customer service team immediately.

Shipping & Returns

How long does shipping take?

Standard shipping takes 5-7 business days. Express shipping (2-3 days) and overnight shipping are also available for additional fees.

What is your return policy?

We offer a 30-day return policy for unworn items in original packaging. Custom pieces and sale items are final sale.

Do you ship internationally?

Yes, we ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location.

Care & Maintenance

How should I care for my jewelry?

Store jewelry in a clean, dry place. Clean with a soft cloth and mild soap. Avoid exposure to chemicals, perfumes, and extreme temperatures. We recommend professional cleaning annually.

Do you offer jewelry cleaning services?

Yes, we offer professional cleaning and inspection services. You can mail your jewelry to us or visit our store for these services.

What is your warranty policy?

We offer a lifetime warranty on all our jewelry covering manufacturing defects. This includes free repairs and stone replacement for defects.
