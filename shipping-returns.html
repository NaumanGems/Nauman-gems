Shipping & Returns

Everything you need to know about our shipping and return policies

Shipping Information

Free Shipping

We offer free standard shipping on all orders over $500. Orders under $500 have a flat rate shipping fee of $15.

Shipping Methods

  • Standard Shipping: 5-7 business days
  • Express Shipping: 2-3 business days ($25)
  • Overnight Shipping: Next business day ($45)

International Shipping

We ship to most countries worldwide. International shipping rates and delivery times vary by location. Duties and taxes are the responsibility of the recipient.

Order Tracking

You'll receive a tracking number via email once your order ships. Track your package through our website or the carrier's website.

Returns & Exchanges

30-Day Return Policy

We accept returns within 30 days of purchase for a full refund or exchange. Items must be unworn, undamaged, and in original packaging.

Return Process

  1. Contact our customer service team
  2. Receive a return authorization number
  3. Ship the item back with the provided label
  4. Refund will be processed within 5-7 business days

Non-Returnable Items

  • Custom or engraved pieces
  • Sale items marked as final sale
  • Items that have been worn or damaged
  • Gift cards

Exchanges

We're happy to exchange items for a different size or style. Exchanges are subject to availability and price differences.

Need Help?

Our customer service team is here to help with any questions about shipping or returns.
